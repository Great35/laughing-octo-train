Our Story

CEO Richard Todd and President Wendy Dominguez Co-founded JanMarek Management in 1996 with a mission of stewardship: creating investment solutions that serve the best interests of clients, as a fiduciary.

The conviction to serve others and create a client-centric firm originated when Rich and Wendy left their Wall Street firm to establish one of the first fee-only investment consulting firms in this region. More than ever, Wall Street firms and insurance providers are encumbered by conflicts of interest and commission-based incentives.

JanMarek Management is recognized as a leader in consulting to retirement plans, nonprofits, and families. In the last 28 years, our number of clients has grown organically because of the caliber of services and solutions we provide.

At JanMarek Management, we have been thoroughly evaluated by many of the largest employers and nonprofit organizations in the country. Their rigorous due diligence process results in expansive reports that outline the JanMarek Management approach, depth, access, philosophies, and company culture. They find a firm with undivided dedication to our clients and consequently we consistently win the business of institutional investors.
